zViz will revolutionize the industry

VFXWorld has posted an article about combining motion capture with previsualization. Their article starts with a nice nod to our work, and includes a good survey of current on set motion capture technologies.
In today's world of high-tech production, realtime, onset previs is becoming a reality. Just how important is it? If you're familiar with ILM's Zviz, you know that George Lucas believes this interactive previs system utilizing the LucasArts game engine will eventually revolutionize the industry.

Today, they continue their apparent series with a few case studies featuring companies doing previz.
