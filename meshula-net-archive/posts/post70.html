Surf's Up Course Notes

Rob Bredow posted his course notes for Surf's Up on his blog. There were a lot of really nice tricks and tips in this presentation; in particular I liked the solution for what do when the the camera dips partially under the water line. The technique for beach breakers was very clever as well, I was particularly impressed with the idea of using a displacement map and a shader to to build up waves on the beach.
