Kaguya

2007-11-18 00:19:59

Kaguya Hime is a 10th century Japanese folk tale about an elderly bamboo-cutter who discovers a tiny baby girl living inside a stalk of bamboo. He takes her home, and with his wife raises the girl. Eventually she becomes a renowned beauty, and is taken home to the capital of the moon. The Japanese lunar probe orbiting the moon now is named Kaguya. The research team has released this stunning video of Earthrise and Earthset over the moon.
