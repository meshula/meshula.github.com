Abandoned Nike base

Out on a recent hike, I stumbled across an abandoned Nike missile base. Three silos welded shut, rusting, filling with water in the rain. An apocalypse that might have been, abandoned to ruin. Mushrooms dotted the site, and the rocks were cleaved through by planes of quartz, giving the whole a mythic feel.
