Weta digital pipeline

The May 2006 issue of CGWorld magazine has an analysis of the Weta digital pipeline. It looks from this that the pipeline is not a fully converged previz/production pipeline. This pipeline has two parallel tracks that are not brought together until compositing. A converged pipeline would share elements in the creation stage.
