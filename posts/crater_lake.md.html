Crater Lake =========== Drifting in a lake of deepest blue
  underneath a sky of deepest blue
The bottom falls out
  and all the way down is all the way up
The bowl of the sky overturns
  the stars are in the lake
Floating, sinking, flying
  all are the same
Swimming, thrashing, pulling, splashing! The lake, the sky, the stars
  all are the same Strangely cold,
  strangely warm And at the last minute
  a rock in the void
Above or below, who knows
  as above so below
Breathe, breathe One pull to shove and the void lets me go Stars recede, sky and lake let me go Breathe, breathe ___ (c) 15 Aug 2009 Nick Porcino
